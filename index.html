01 — About

I'm a software engineering graduate making a deliberate transition into tech after 15 years serving in the U.S. Army and as a Paramedic Lieutenant. That background isn't a detour — it's a foundation.

I've operated in environments where accuracy, documentation, and fast decisions under pressure aren't best practices — they're requirements. I bring that same standard to software: security-first, modular, and built to hold up.

"The same discipline that carried me through war zones and emergency calls also carried me through debugging code at two in the morning."

Father of five daughters. Army veteran. Paramedic. Developer. I chose this path not because it was easy, but because it offered something I hadn't felt in years: the chance to create, solve problems with purpose, and chart a future that was mine to define.
